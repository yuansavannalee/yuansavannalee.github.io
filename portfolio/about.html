A Little About Me

Hi, I am Yuan (Savanna) Li.

I am currently a master student specialized in Human-Computer Interaction, at the University of Michigan’s School of Information. Before the school, I worked as educator, journalist and communication specialist in WWF (World Wildlife Fund). Please refer to the resume to know more about me.

Life is an exciting journey with all dots connected. All the past experience of program management, the empathy cultivated by teaching, the story telling skills trained by news reporting, and the passion for innovation will lead me to my destination— an excellent UX designer.

I love painting, sewing, cooking, comic books and video games. And of course, I love DESIGN! I am going to have a new section called "inspiration" in my website, to share with you my work of these hobbies, my reflection about design in everyday life, as well as my take-aways after reading some UX blogs and research reports.

Thanks for visiting my website, and pls feel free to reach me if you have anything interesting to talk with me.
